Where the magic of performance meets the art of dining

Every Friday · Summer Season

Cabaret Night

Every Friday evening, the grand dome of Circé transforms into an intimate cabaret. Burlesque dancers, magicians, and live performers take the stage while you savour a gastronomic dinner crafted by Chef Jean Christiansen.

The luxurious, velvet-draped setting creates an atmosphere of mystery and elegance — a true homage to the enchantress Circé herself. Live DJ sets carry the evening through to the early hours.

Doors open at 7:00 PM · Dinner service from 8:00 PM · Show at 9:30 PM

Every Saturday · Summer Season

Saturday Dinner Show

An evening where spectacle and gastronomy meet in the most sumptuous of settings. The Belle Époque dome hall transforms into a modern cabaret stage, alive with performers, dancers, singers, and artists.

Each Saturday presents a unique programme, curated to surprise and delight. A multi-course dinner accompanies the performance, with dishes designed to complement each act — a feast for all the senses.

From 7:30 PM · Five-course dinner with show

Annual Summer Event

The White Party

Our iconic summer celebration — an unforgettable night of elegance, music, and Mediterranean magic under the Riviera sky. Dress code: all white.

The terrace and gardens are transformed for one extraordinary evening of dancing, live performances, gourmet bites, and flowing champagne, with the sea and Cap-Ferrat as a backdrop. The White Party has become one of the most anticipated events on the Côte d'Azur social calendar.

Date announced each spring · By invitation & reservation
