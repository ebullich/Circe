The Experience

A Pearl of the Côte d'Azur

In the heart of Beaulieu-sur-Mer, Circé offers a unique culinary experience inspired by the enchantress of Greek mythology.

Perched on the heights of the port, this majestic edifice offers breathtaking views of the sea and Cap-Ferrat, with sunsets illuminating the Baie des Fourmis. Beneath the grand dome, amid Italian marble and shimmering chandeliers, Chef Jean Christiansen elevates Mediterranean flavours with creative, refined, and seasonal cuisine.

Step onto the jasmine-covered garden terrace, cocktail in hand, and let the sound of live DJs carry you into the evening. Circé is more than a restaurant — it is a destination.

The Chef

Jean Christiansen

An artisan of Mediterranean flavours who, with passion, elevates each ingredient to create unique dishes.

Chef Christiansen's cuisine showcases the freshest, finest local produce from the Mediterranean. His creative approach blends traditional techniques with modern innovation, resulting in dishes that are both deeply rooted in the region and refreshingly original.

Whether for an intimate dinner for two, a special celebration, or a gathering with friends, each plate tells a story of the sea, the sun, and the soil of Provence.
